At OVERBOUGHT.COM you will save money, you will re-utilize the under-utilized, and you will decrease the strain on our landfills. A quick inventory of most homes will uncover items for which there is no longer a need nor storage space; parts for now replaced appliances, items such as car parts for vehicles we no longer own(belts, oil/air filters), bags of gizmos from the box store down the street (you couldn't buy just the one you needed), or maybe you bought the pack of six because it was almost as cheap as the pack of two. At OVERBOUGHT.COM you can de-clutter your home, earn shipping credits, and, before making that next purchase, save money by searching our database first. Links to online retailers are included so that you can see what you saved or, if your search was unsuccessful, assist you in acquiring what you need.

Become an Overbought member today. When you wish to offer an item for reuse just enter a short description, the items dimensions and weight, and any available id/serial numbers. When possible, help your fellow members by uploading a picture of the offered item. If there is a request for your offer, we will make the transaction as easy and as anonymous as possible. The requester pays for all postage and handling. Just insert the item into the pre-paid, labeled container delivered to your residence. Then return the container to your mail carrier. For each successful item shared you earn a credit that can be used to offset mailing costs on the next item that you yourself find in our database.
